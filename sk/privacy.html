Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the GDPR and other applicable laws.

1. Data Controller

This website is maintained by Ondřej Výšek, located in the Czech Republic.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect data that you voluntarily provide to us, specifically your email address when you sign up for the 30-Day Challenge or our newsletter.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your email address solely to send you the daily challenge prompts and, if you opted in, news about Copilot Chat. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) as our email marketing platform. By submitting your email address, you acknowledge that the information you provide will be transferred to Brevo for processing in accordance with their Terms of Use.

Brevo is GDPR compliant and servers are located within the EU.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time by clicking the link in the footer of our emails.

6. Cookies

We use only necessary cookies to ensure the proper functioning of the website. We do not use tracking cookies without your explicit consent.

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at ondrej@vysek.cz.
